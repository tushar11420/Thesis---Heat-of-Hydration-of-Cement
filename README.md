This repository contains most of my works of UG Thesis. The thesis is about the quantification heat of Hydration by Expermental set-up and
simulation with a specialized software called NIST VCCTL. You will have a clear idea by reading my abstract.

All the data from Experiments are standardized and cleaned by matlab, then measure the specific heat generation rate by numerical differentiation. Ultimately, 
calculate the amount of heat by numerical integration. All things are done through matlab. You will all my matlab code and necessary excel files with data in 
the directory called, "Final Part".
" 
Theory of the methodology followed to calculate cumulative heat of hydration of cement is in "Theory of the Lab-work".

You will find Software output and other necessary simulation files for specific cement in "Final Part" inside the the files CEM I..., CEM II A-M...., CEM II 
B-M... etc.

Data and method of calibration about our set-up will be found in the directory called Calibration. 

I have submitted two abstract based on my thesis work in ICCE BUET, ICACE KUET conference.  ICACE KUET have already accepted my abstract! you will have my 
drafts in the directory called " conference Submission".
